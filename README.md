# Urban Company NEXT 🚀

An AI-powered product redesign prototype for Urban Company focused on improving customer retention, cross-service adoption, and monetization through predictive home intelligence.

## 📌 Project Overview

Urban Company NEXT transforms the traditional service booking experience into a proactive home management platform by introducing an AI assistant that predicts maintenance needs, recommends relevant services, and enables seamless bookings.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many users interact with Urban Company only when an immediate need arises, resulting in low repeat engagement and limited cross-service adoption.

## 💡 Solution

Urban Company NEXT introduces an AI-powered Home Assistant that provides:

- Predictive maintenance reminders
- Personalized service recommendations
- Smart booking assistance
- Home health dashboard
- AI-powered insights for proactive home care

## 🔍 Research

- Conducted **40 survey responses**
- Conducted **4 user interviews**
- Built user personas
- Identified customer pain points across the service journey
- Evaluated retention and monetization opportunities

## 📈 North Star Metric

**Monthly AI-Assisted Bookings**

Supporting Metrics:
- Cross-Service Adoption Rate
- Repeat Booking Rate
- AI Recommendation Click-Through Rate
